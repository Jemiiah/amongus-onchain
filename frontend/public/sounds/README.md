# Game Audio

Place your audio files (`.mp3` or `.wav`) in this directory.

## Sound Effects (`/sounds/`)

### Main Events
- `body-reported.mp3` - Alarm sound when a dead body is discovered
- `emergency-meeting.mp3` - Alert/siren for emergency meetings
- `ejection.mp3` - Whoosh/space sound when player is ejected
- `kill.mp3` - Sound effect when a kill occurs

### Game State
- `victory.mp3` - Victory fanfare when you win
- `defeat.mp3` - Defeat sound when you lose
- `game-start.mp3` - Sound when the game begins

### Voting
- `vote-start.mp3` - Sound when voting phase begins
- `vote-cast.mp3` - Click/confirmation sound when vote is submitted

### Tasks & UI
- `task-complete.mp3` - Ding/success sound when a task is completed
- `button-click.mp3` - UI click sound (optional)

---

## Background Music (`/sounds/music/`)

Looping background tracks for different game phases:

- `menu.mp3` - Main menu ambient music (loops)
- `lobby.mp3` - Waiting room/lobby music (loops)
- `gameplay.mp3` - In-game suspenseful music (loops)
- `voting.mp3` - Tense discussion/voting music (loops)
- `victory.mp3` - Victory celebration music (plays once)
- `defeat.mp3` - Defeat/loss music (plays once)

### Music Guidelines
- **Format**: MP3 (recommended for compression)
- **Length**: 1-3 minutes for looping tracks
- **Loop Points**: Ensure seamless loop transitions
- **Volume**: Normalize to similar levels, keep quieter than sound effects

## Recommended Sources

You can find royalty-free game audio at:
- [Freesound.org](https://freesound.org)
- [OpenGameArt.org](https://opengameart.org)
- [Mixkit](https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/game/)
- [Incompetech](https://incompetech.com) - Kevin MacLeod's music

## Format Guidelines

- **Format**: MP3 (recommended) or WAV
- **Sound Effects Length**: Keep short (0.5-3 seconds)
- **Music Length**: 1-3 minutes for looping tracks
- **Sample Rate**: 44.1kHz recommended
- **Volume**: Normalize levels across all audio files
